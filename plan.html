Goals

The Cossieplay site is designed to build revenue for Cossieplay through attracting new customers, increasing sales and the attendance of events.

Success Evaluation

After a 12 week period a success evaluation will be applicable. Purchases, customers and event participation will be calculated and compared to exsisting data and any increase will be attributed to new site.

Target Audience

The site will focus on high school students and young adults. To target theses age groups the sites content will favor imagery and limit the amount of paragraphs opting for more key points and short blocks of text.

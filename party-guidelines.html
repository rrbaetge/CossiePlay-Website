Party Guidelines

CossiePlay party-goers party at their own risk and are responsible for their own safety.

Check with your doctor before you party to hard.

Party fees must be paid at the door. Cash or EFTPOS accepted. Credit card payments attract a 2% surcharge.

Alcohol is only served at the bar to Cosplayers who are 18 or over only.

No intoxicated persons will be served, but will be escorted to a taxi

Which will be paid by the party-goer.
